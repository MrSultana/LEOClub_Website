Fundraising Events

The LEO Club carries out many fundraising activities over the course of a year. Some of these are:

  • Valentine Day Balloons
  • Hire a Helper
  • Ball Extravaganza
  • Old/foreign coins to turn into cash
  • Plus many more

Our Ball Extravaganza is our big event for the year and on average we make in excess of $4,000. We target different organisations to receive this money. Last year it was Transplant House Auckland and this year it is Motor Neuron Southland.
Every second year we are really involved in the Relay for Life and this raises money for Cancer Awarness. Relay For Life is an overnight walking event that brings teams of friends, work colleagues and families together in a fun, carnival like atmosphere to fundraise for the Cancer Society. James Hargest College is proud to support this event.
